Rebecca Palmer is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ Professional and the chief organizer of a busy family based in Los Angeles. She knows that the juggle is real! She and her husband are Silicon Valley transplants and never tire of building things (businesses and blanket forts)!She likes helping people resolve their mental clutter and relieve financial stress. You might be wondering: Am I doing it right? Am I doing enough? She works with members to navigate those decisions and set up their financial lives to operate on autopilot—so they can think less about money and more about living life. She works with young families, start-up founders, creatives, and everything in between. Prior to Fruitful, she ran a pro bono financial practice, wrote a book about financial planning, and worked at Ellevest and in private wealth management in the Bay Area.
