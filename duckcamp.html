Elevating the Outdoor Shopping Experience

As a Senior Product Designer, I led the transformative redesign of Duck Camp's e-commerce platform, blending traditional retail with innovative, user-centric design. I aimed to enrich the online shopping experience, focusing on customer education and decision-making, particularly for hunting gear choices based on specific pursuits and technical specifications.

Role

I led the comprehensive overhaul of Duck Camp's e-commerce platform, enhancing the user experience through an interactive system builder and detailed product weight info to increase cart size and enrich the shopping experience. My role also involved strategic growth, expanding women's products, and editorial content.

Problem

Duck Camp's challenge was incrementally improving the e-commerce platform to engage users and improve conversion rates. The goal was to create an intuitive and rich online experience catering to hunting enthusiasts' unique preferences and requirements.

Solution

To tackle Duck Camp's challenges, I created an Interactive System Builder for outfit customization, detailed product weight information for informed choices, and inclusive marketing modules for the women's product line, enhancing user experience and aligning with brand ethos.

Navigating the Wilderness & Impact of Design: Duck Camp's Project Journey

At the outset of my collaboration with Duck Camp, I prioritized understanding their observed challenges and objectives. This began with a thorough analysis and audit of their existing e-commerce platform to pinpoint opportunities for enhancement. I identified and provided actionable solutions by conducting a focused heuristic evaluation, especially in areas they sought to improve. This approach addressed the immediate issues Duck Camp faced and aligned with their broader goals, forming the basis for my engagement and assistance in refining their online shopping experience.

New Product Content Modules The homepage received a complete makeover, introducing new product image styling, carousel interaction, and all shop categorization functionality. This redesign was about refreshing the look and improving product discoverability and user navigation, making the online journey more intuitive and engaging for our customers.

Enhancing User Friendliness The comprehensive update to the system navigation was a significant part of the project. This effort aimed to streamline the user's journey through the site, ensuring a more seamless and user-friendly experience from the homepage to checkout.

Versatile Integration Across the Site I developed a variety of marketing modules specifically for new product lines. These modules were crafted with versatility, enabling their integration across different site sections and enhancing the visibility and appeal of new products.

"Wear it with" Suggestion Modulee A standout feature of the redesign was the Vantage System (Kit) Builder, a "Wear it with" suggestion module. This innovative tool allows users to mix and match products easily, offering an intuitive modular experience that enhances outfit selection across various categories and products.

These strategic enhancements contributed to a more dynamic, efficient, and enjoyable shopping experience at Duck Camp. My role in this comprehensive project showcased a harmonious blend of technical proficiency, creative design, and strategic foresight, all directed toward reinforcing Duck Camp's position as a leader in the outdoor e-commerce industry.

Project FAQs

  • My approach emphasized collaborative leadership. I worked closely with various teams, including marketing for the women's product line, ensuring that each group's insights contributed to the final design. This collaborative process was crucial in achieving a cohesive and successful redesign.

  • Success was measured through increased cart size, improved conversion rates, and positive customer feedback. These metrics indicated that the redesign met and exceeded our goals, highlighting the effectiveness of the new features and overall UX improvements.

  • My work directly contributed to Duck Camp's growth by improving the online shopping experience, which led to increased sales and customer satisfaction. Introducing features catering to a broader demographic, including women, also helped expand the brand's market reach.
