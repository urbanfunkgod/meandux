New Dimensions: The Virtuix Revolution in VR Gaming

At Virtuix, I significantly contributed to developing the Omni One, a unique omnidirectional VR platform poised to be the Peloton of VR gaming. My role involved innovating beyond traditional design, focusing on an immersive user experience that leverages the Omni One's unique capabilities for natural movement in VR, blending gaming with physical activity. This project, integrating advanced hardware with a dynamic online community, aimed to revolutionize VR gaming, making it accessible and engaging. The Omni One has set new standards in the VR industry, merging interactive entertainment with fitness and paving the way for future immersive experiences.

Role

As the lead Product Designer for the Omni One VR platform, my role encompassed gathering requirements, guiding brand identity, and architecting system design. I played a crucial role in defining and executing the VR experience, collaborating closely with and leading an offshore development team to bring our vision to fruition.

Problem

The initial challenge of the Virtuix project involved clarifying vague concepts into a concrete vision for Omni One. My role included leading workshops for gap analysis and concept clarification, which were pivotal in crafting a strategic roadmap for the product's development.

Solution

Developed a streamlined operating system for Virtuix Omni One featuring a user-friendly Game Store and an integrated iOS app for efficient game queuing and headset installation. Also established an internal game development and publishing portal, ensuring a unified VR ecosystem.
